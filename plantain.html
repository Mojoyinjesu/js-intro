Fried Plantain

Ingredients

  • Plantain
  • Groundnut oil or Palm Oil
  • Salt(optional)

Instructions

  1. Peel plantains: cut off both ends, score lengthwise, and peel. Slice into rounds (½ inch) or on the diagonal for larger pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat until shimmering (test with a small piece — it should sizzle).
  3. Carefully add plantain slices in a single layer (don't overcrowd). Fry on medium heat for 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown and caramelized. Adjust heat so they cook through without burning.
  4. Use a slotted spoon to remove and drain on paper towels. Lightly salt if desired.
  5. Serve hot as a side or snack.

Serve with:

  • Jollof rice
  • Fried rice
