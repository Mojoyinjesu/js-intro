Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • vegetable oil
  • Seasoning(curry powder, thyme, bay leaves, salt, seasonsing cube, garlic)
  • Stock
  • rice, carrots, peas, green beans, shrimp, gizzard,
  • Onions, peppers(habanero)

Instructions

  1. Cook rice for about 70-80% doneness (so it’s firm, not mushy). Drain and spread rice on a tray to cool, best if made ahead and chilled (helps separate grains).
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in large skillet or wok, scramble beaten eggs quickly, break into pieces, remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add remaining oil. Sauté chopped onion until fragrant. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds.
  4. Add peas & carrots, corn, diced green pepper. Stir-fry 2-3 minutes until just tender. Add cooked protein and heat through.
  5. Increase heat to medium-high. Add chilled rice to the pan, gently tossing to combine with vegetables. Break up clumps with spatula.
  6. Sprinkle curry powder, thyme,salt & pepper, and add soy sauce if using. Keep tossing so rice fries evenly and gets slightly toasted.

Serve with:

  • Fried Plantain
  • Coleslaw
