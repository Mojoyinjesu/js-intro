Amala

Ingredients

  • Amala flour(yam flour or cassava flour)
  • Hot Water

Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil. Pour the boiling water into a deep pot or bowl.
  2. Add yam flour gradually while stirring quickly with wooden spatula or strong spoon. You can also sprinkle some flour and keep stirring to avoid lumps.
  3. Keep stirring and turning the Amala in the pot until it forms a thick, smooth, elastic dough. Add small amounts of flour or water to adjust firmness.
  4. Remove from heat, shape into portions and serve with soups like ewedu, egusi, or gbegiri.

Serve with:

  • Egusi Soup
