Fufu

Ingredients

  • Cassava flour
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Peel cassava, cut into chunks, remove woody core.
  2. Boil until very soft (30-40 minutes). Drain.
  3. While hot, mash thoroughly with a mortar & pestle or use a sturdy blender/food processor to pound into a smooth, stretchy dough. Some people pass through a sieve to remove fibres.
  4. Return to low heat pot and stir continuously until it becomes elastic and smooth. Shape and serve.

Serve with:

  • Egusi Soup
