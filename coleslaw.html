Coleslaw

Ingredients

  • Mayonaise
  • Shredded carrot and cabbage
  • Lemon juice
  • Honey
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Place shredded cabbage and carrots in a large bowl.
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, yogurt (if using), lemon juice, honey, salt and pepper until smooth. Taste and adjust
  3. Pour dressing over vegetables and toss until evenly coated.
  4. Cover and chill at least 30 minutes to let flavors marry (best if chilled 1-2 hours).
  5. Toss again before serving and adjust seasoning if needed.

Serve with:

  • Jollof rice
  • Fried rice
