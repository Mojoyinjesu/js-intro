Eba

Ingredients

  • Garri
  • hot water

Instructions

  1. Boil about 3 cups of water or heat until steaming. Remove from heat
  2. Pour the hot water into a deep bowl or mortar/reservoir for mixing.
  3. Pour the garri slowly into the water while stirring continuously with a spoon until it thickens to your preferred firmness. Start with about half the garri, stir, then add more to reach the right texture.
  4. Once it forms a smooth, cohesive dough-like mass, use the spoon or a wooden pounder to fold and press, this removes lumps and makes it smooth.
  5. Wet your hands or a damp spoon and shape into balls or portions. Serve hot with soups (egusi, ogbono, etc.) or stews.

Serve with:

  • Egusi Soup
