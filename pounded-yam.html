Pounded Yam

Ingredients

  • Boileed water
  • Yam

Instructions

  1. Peel and wash yams; cut into even chunks
  2. Place yam in a pot, cover with water, and bring to a boil. Cook until yams are tender (a fork slides through easily), 20-25 minutes depending on size.
  3. Drain water and transfer hot yams to a mortar and pestle or a strong wooden bowl. Pound vigorously until smooth and stretchy with no lumps. This requires effort and rhythm.
  4. Shape into portions and serve immediately with soup (egusi, ogbono, etc.).

Serve with:

  • Egusi Soup
